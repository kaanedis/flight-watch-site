Flight Watch

A personal flight price tracker — get an email the moment the cheapest fare on your route changes.

Airfare on some routes swings dramatically within hours: a ticket listed at 2.100 TL in the morning can cost 3.300 TL the same evening. Flight Watch exists to catch the cheap window before it closes.

How it works

  1. You pick the routes and travel dates you care about.
  2. An automated job checks fares for those routes around the clock, comparing prices across airlines.
  3. When the cheapest fare drops, hits a new low, or starts climbing, you get an email with the best current offers — until you buy your ticket.

What it is (and isn't)

Flight Watch is a personal, non-commercial tool: no accounts, no ads, no audience — just automated price monitoring for its owner's own trips, with a full price history kept per route. Fare data comes from public flight search APIs, and alerts link straight to the flight search so every price can be verified before booking.
